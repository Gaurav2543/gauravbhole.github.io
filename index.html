Research Focus

My work is centered on applying deep learning techniques to pressing challenges in life sciences, with a special focus on medical imaging, genomics, and computational biology.

Deep Learning in Life Sciences

Leveraging architectures like CNNs, Transformers, and GNNs to model complex biological data and uncover novel insights.

Medical Imaging Analysis

Developing robust models for tasks like classification, segmentation, and detection in mammography and other imaging modalities.

Genetics & Structural Variants

Analyzing long-read sequencing data to accurately detect structural variants and understand their role in human genetic diversity and disease.
